  • Sunday 16 May 2010: Chenla at the WorldBeat, Concert & Fundraiser

    Join us at the The WorldBeat Center in Balboa Park, San Diego, California for an afternoon of Music, Dance and of course Beer. Performances provided by Domingosieste from Los Angeles & La Santa Cecilia and the PLopez Project. This event will kick off Chenla's San Diego Plus-or-Minus 40 collection of San Diego's history in Images, Video & Words of San Diegans. Everyone is encouraged to bring copies of their own Images, Audio and Video to be added to the collection.
