Presentations

Starting in 2013 as part of our commitment to using only FOSS and open standards, all of our presentations will by created in emacs using org-export-to-s5, which generates a HTML5 presentation.

For best results use a modern browser like Firefox or Google-Chrome and hit the F11 key to go full screen. Use the right and left arrow keys to navigate the presentation, and then hit F11 again to escape full screen mode.

All presentations listed on this page are released under a CC BY Creative Commons Attribution only licence. You are free to copy and distribute them so long as you tell people where it came from.
