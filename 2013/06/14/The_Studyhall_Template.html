The Studyhall Template

Posted on 14 Jun 2013 by Brad Collins

We have just updated the Studyhall Template Project Page. We have been putting a lot of work into the studyhall template concept over the last six months. Our original goal was to establish the first studyhall in Vientiane, Laos, which was only an hour's drive from where I was living in Udon Thani, Thailand at the time. We still intend to eventually open a studyhall in Vientiane, but it won't be our first. Our plans abruptly changed when I was lured to Phnom Penh with the enticement of a potentially lucrative unrelated project that didn't pan out. But in the process I found that I had permanently relocated to Cambodia.

So our first studyhall will be built in Phnom Penh, near the Tonle & Mekong rivers. We have been approaching government ministries, local NGO's (and there are a lot of NGO's in Cambodia) and local universities to get their reaction and feedback to the idea. The response has been extremely positive. So we are now putting together a formal business plan and detailed budget and will be establishing ourselves as what I call an un-NGO.
