Plan Cddr: Backup, Primer & Restore

Posted on 19 Jun 2013 by Brad Collins

Just uploaded Plan Cddr: Backup, Primer & Restore. Plan Cddr is Chenla's Big Vision, that pulls together all the work we've done going back to 1997. The Burr Metadata Framework, was developed as the data structure for the Backup. The Saltmine Server software that we are developing will manage very long term archives and libraries encoded in BMF. And Studyhall Template provides a base unit for establishing educational programs, research and development communities to build a global, multi-lingual, distributed federated network of very long term archives and libraries.

We would love to hear a from you. Since comments on the site are still down, send email to Brad or Ruben.
