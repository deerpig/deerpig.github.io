Privacy Policy

All user data that is not made public, including email addresses and IPs associated with user accounts, will not be shared with outside parties for any reason unless compelled by law or where reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property, and well-being of Chenla. Chenla does keep machine logs but only for a time necessary to maintain the site. When a visitor requests or reads a page, or sends email to an Chenla server, no more information is collected than is typically collected by web sites.

Chenla believes that maintaining and preserving the privacy of user data is an important value. This Privacy Policy, together with other policies, resolutions, and actions by Chenla, represent a committed effort to safeguard the security of the limited user information that is collected and retained on our servers. Nevertheless, Chenla cannot guarantee that user information will remain private. We acknowledge that, in spite of our committed effort to protect private user information, determined individuals may still develop data-mining and other methods to uncover such information and disclose it. For this reason, Chenla can make no guarantee against unauthorized access to information provided in the course of using this website.
