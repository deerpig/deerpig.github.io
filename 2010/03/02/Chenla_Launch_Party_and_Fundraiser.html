Chenla Launch Party and Fundraiser

Posted on 13 Jun 2014 by Brad Collins

You are invited to join us on Friday March 19, in San Diego for the Chenla Launch Party & Fundraiser. There will be presentations introducing our initial projects including The Mekong Studyhall Project, The Burr Metadata Framework and San Diego: Plus-or-Minus 40 which will be available as videos online after the part. But don't worry, we will also have food drink and music. Details and links to register can be found on the event page for the party. Please pass this on to anyone you think might be interested. The official hashtag for the event (on Twitter) is #chenlalaunch. And if you haven't already, please follow us on twitter.com/chenlala. We look forward to seeing you there!
