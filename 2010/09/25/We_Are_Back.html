We're Back

Posted on 16 Jun 2014 by Brad Collins

It's been a rough few months since the concert in May, but we're back. We may not have been posting, but we've been working hard on a number of ideas and projects which were hinted at in the presentation we gave in March.

Brad will be moving back to Bangkok in October. We hope to be giving a presenation at BarCamp Bangkok in late October. And work will begin in earnest on the Saltmine server prototype in November.
